MAURITIUS MASS FINDER — v24.8 NEAR ME RANKING FIX VERIFICATION REPORT

Verdict: PASS — safe for public release now

Root cause found:
- Typed queries such as “près de moi” and “near me” were not routed deterministically into the Near Me workflow.
- Near Me sorting prioritised distance before next Mass timing. This could make a close church with a Mass several days away outrank a sooner Mass.
- When location was unavailable, the app could still continue into a misleading result state rather than showing a safe location-required fallback.

Fixes implemented:
- Added accent-insensitive Near Me intent detection for French and English phrases: près de moi, pres de moi, proche de moi, autour de moi, à côté de moi, dans les environs, near me, nearby, closest, nearest, around me.
- Typed Near Me queries now trigger the same geolocation path as the Near Me button.
- Ordinary text-search scoring is bypassed when Near Me mode is active.
- Near Me ranking is now deterministic: days until next Mass, minutes until next Mass, distance, coordinate precision, then site name.
- If location is unavailable, no fake “near me” result is shown. The app displays a bilingual fallback.
- Added Near Me result heading and warning for cases where the nearest useful Mass is more than 2 days away.
- Updated app version, version.json, service-worker cache, manifest, README and changelog to v24.8.

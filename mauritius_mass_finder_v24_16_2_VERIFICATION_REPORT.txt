Mauritius Mass Finder v24.16.2 — Verification Report

Verdict: PASS — safe to deploy.

Root cause fixed:
The previous Next Mass badge used elapsed minutes to decide whether to display Today/Aujourd’hui. A Mass less than 24 hours away but falling on the next civil calendar day could therefore be labelled Today/Aujourd’hui incorrectly.

Fix implemented:
- Added civil-date based next occurrence calculation.
- Added calendarDaysUntil() and relativeDayLabel().
- Next/near result day badge now compares the Mass occurrence date to today’s local calendar date, not to a 24-hour duration bucket.
- Added EN label: Tomorrow.
- Added FR label: Demain.
- Preserved relative duration display such as “in 23h45 / dans 23 h 45 min”.

Expected behavior after fix:
- If it is Thursday 16:30 and the next Mass is Friday 16:15, the app may show “dans 23 h 45 min”, but the date badge shows “Demain”, not “Aujourd’hui”.
- Same-day future Masses still show Today / Aujourd’hui.
- Masses two or more civil days away show In n day(s) / Dans n jour(s).

Residual risk:

This is acceptable for the current static app architecture but could be refined later by forcing Indian/Mauritius timezone calculation.

Final decision:
Deploy v24.16.2. Do not deploy v24.16.1 if this Next Mass label issue matters for public trust.
